Privacy Policy

Effective Date: January 28, 2026

Last Updated: January 28, 2026

1. Introduction

NapTap ("we", "our", or "the app") is a power nap timer application. We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we handle your information when you use NapTap.

2. Information We Collect

NapTap does not collect any personal information.

All data generated by the app is stored locally on your device and is never transmitted to any server, third party, or external service. Specifically:

  • Nap History: Your nap records (date, time, duration) are stored locally on your device using SQLite. This data never leaves your device.
  • Settings: Your app preferences (sound, vibration toggles) are stored locally using SharedPreferences.
  • Step Data: Step counting data from your device's motion sensors is used in real-time to count steps for the walk-to-dismiss feature. This data is not stored or transmitted.

3. Permissions We Use

NapTap requests the following device permissions:

  • Motion & Fitness (iOS) / Activity Recognition (Android): Used solely to count steps for the walk-to-dismiss alarm feature. Step data is processed in real-time and not stored or shared.
  • Notifications: Used to display the wake-up alarm notification when your nap timer ends.
  • Foreground Service (Android): Used to keep the nap timer running while the screen is off or the app is in the background.
  • Vibration: Used for haptic feedback during the alarm.

4. Data Sharing

We do not share any data with third parties. NapTap does not contain any analytics, tracking, advertising SDKs, or any form of data collection. There are no user accounts, no cloud storage, and no network requests for data collection purposes.

5. Data Storage & Security

All app data is stored locally on your device using standard platform storage mechanisms (SQLite database and SharedPreferences). Data is protected by your device's built-in security features (device lock, encryption).

6. Data Deletion

Since all data is stored locally on your device, you can delete all NapTap data by:

  • Uninstalling the app (removes all data)
  • Clearing the app's data through your device settings

7. Children's Privacy

NapTap is not directed at children under 13. The app does not collect any personal information from anyone, including children.

8. Third-Party Services

NapTap does not use any third-party services that collect user data. The app may make a one-time network request to load a fallback alarm sound if the bundled sound file fails to load. No user data is transmitted in this request.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page with an updated "Last Updated" date.
